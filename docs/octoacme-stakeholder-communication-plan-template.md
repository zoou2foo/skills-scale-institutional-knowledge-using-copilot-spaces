## Communication Principles

- **Timely**: Send communications before stakeholders feel the need to ask for updates.
- **Concise**: Respect stakeholders' time — lead with the key message, then provide detail.
- **Consistent**: Use the agreed templates and channels to reduce confusion.
- **Actionable**: Clearly state what you need from the recipient, and by when.
- **Accessible**: Ensure communications are understandable to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.
